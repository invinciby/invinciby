### 👨‍🎓 About Me
I'm a researcher working at the intersection of **Reinforcement Learning**, **LLM Agents**, and **Scientific Discovery**. My long-term goal is to build **self-improving agents** that learn to reason, use tools, and solve open-ended scientific problems through interaction — not just imitation.

> 专注于智能体强化学习（Agentic RL）方向，致力于让 LLM 智能体通过交互学会推理、调用工具、解决开放式科学问题。

- 🔭 **Currently exploring** — Agentic RL for multi-turn tool use and long-horizon reasoning (RLVR / GRPO / DPO / PRM)
- ⚗️ **Domain testbed** — Chemistry & Materials Discovery, a natural playground for verifiable-reward RL
- 🧩 **Belief** — The next leap in agents comes from **environment design + reward modeling**, not just bigger models
- 📝 **Writing** — Sharing notes on agents & RL at [CSDN](https://blog.csdn.net/VinciB) and WeChat OA **「你好不吃虾」**

---

### 🧪 Research Interests
**🎯 Core: Agentic Reinforcement Learning | 核心方向：智能体强化学习**

- Training LLM agents with RL for **multi-turn tool use**, **planning**, and **self-correction**
- **Verifiable rewards (RLVR)** and process reward models (PRM) for reasoning tasks
- **Exploration vs. exploitation** in LLM agents — curiosity, uncertainty, diverse rollouts
- **Scaling agentic RL** — rollout efficiency, credit assignment, off-policy methods


**🛠️ Adjacent Directions | 相关方向**

- **Tool-augmented reasoning** — when and how agents should call tools, retrieve, or simulate
- **Agent benchmarking** — evaluation beyond success rate (efficiency, robustness, generalization)
- **AI for Science** — chemistry / materials as high-signal environments for agent training
